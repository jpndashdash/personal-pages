He is the lead designer and developer of travel forecasting tools, including risk analysis methodologies, for the California High-Speed Rail Ridership and Revenue Forecasting model. Dr. Newman received a Doctorate in Civil Engineering from Northwestern University, as well as a Master of Public Administration and a Bachelor of Science degree in Policy Analysis from Cornell University.
